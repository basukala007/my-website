About Me

Principal Architect at Sustainable Buildscape, part-time faculty at Khwopa Engineering College, and an independent design voice in Nepal's built landscape for over fifteen years.

My work spans the full spectrum of architectural practice — master planning, institutional design, healthcare facilities, educational campuses, hospitality, and civic infrastructure. I bring both design sensitivity and technical rigour to every project, informed by a Master's in Construction Management and a Bachelor of Architecture from Pulchowk Campus, T.U.

Alongside practice, I teach architectural design studios at Khwopa Engineering College, believing that the next generation of Nepali architects should be grounded in both craft and critical thinking. I am equally at home in collaborative consortium environments and in the focused intensity of independent freelance work.
